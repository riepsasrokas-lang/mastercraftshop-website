The maker

Light crafted with care, every time

"I knew I wanted my son to be happy — so what better than to lead by example?"

Martin left a career in finance to start Mastercraft from a small workshop in Klaipėda, Lithuania. Nine years later, over 6 000 lights have shipped to homes across the world — each one cut, assembled and packed by hand. Every listing accepts custom sizes, cord lengths and small adjustments. If you have an idea, Martin replies quickly.
